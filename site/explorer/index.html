Blocks

The live blocklace DAG from federation consensus — each block chains to its predecessor (real prev_hash linkage), rendered via <dregg-block-dag>. Click any block (or search a height) to open it in <dregg-block> below.

Cells

Agents in the federation ledger, via <dregg-cell-list> / <dregg-cell>.

Turns

Committed turns share their hash with receipts; <dregg-turn> renders a turn's actions and authorization.

Receipts

The receipt chain via <dregg-receipt-list>; open one for <dregg-receipt> + <dregg-witnessed-receipt> with its trust-tier proof view.

Capabilities

Capability tokens held by the node cipherclerk, via <dregg-capability-list>.

Intents

The node's intent pool. Select one to open the platform <dregg-intent> inspector.

Federation

Known federations + committee via <dregg-federation-list> / <dregg-federation>.

Activity

Live observability feed via <dregg-activity> (node /observability/stream SSE).
