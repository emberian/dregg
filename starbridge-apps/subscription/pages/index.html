Pyana Subscription

Publisher/consumer message queues whose invariants (MonotonicSequence on head/tail, Immutable on capacity + owner, Monotonic on roots + message_root, SenderAuthorized against published publisher / consumer sets) live in a FactoryDescriptor in starbridge-apps/subscription/src/lib.rs. The executor enforces them on every turn.

Storage-as-cell-programs proof: this app replaces the operator-side storage::programmable::ProgrammableQueue enforcement loop with the cell-program substrate. See README.md for the side-by-side comparison.
